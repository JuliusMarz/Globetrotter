🌍 Globetrotters

Rulebook

Welcome to our friendly little Globetrotters community! 🌍
This is a space to explore the world, and share your favorite travel (or everyday) photos.

🌍 How it works
  • Each round, one player is the Tourguide and shares a photo.
  • All other players are Travelers and try to guess the location.
  • The closer your guess, the more points you earn.
🧭 For Travelers
  • You have 24 hours to submit your guess.
  • Enter your guess directly on the map in the Current Round section.
  • You may change your guess once within 1 hour after submitting.
  • You can earn up to 5000 points per round.
  • Every 200m distance = -1 point (0 points beyond 1000km).
  • You may request a Bonusview:
    • -500 points for you
    • +200 points for the Tourguide
    • Score cannot drop below 0
📸 For Tourguides
  • The photo must be taken by you.
  • It must be no older than 1 week.
  • The location must be findable on Google Street View (within ~100m)
    (exceptions: countries without Street View, e.g. China).
  • You may provide one Bonusview:
    • Must be from a similar location
    • Must provide meaningful additional clues
  • Your score per round:
    • Best guess points − worst guess points
    • +200 points per Bonusview usage
  • Inactive player bonus:
      • (5000 / total players) per inactive Traveler (Capped at 2000 points per round)
🏆 Rankings
  • Updated after every round
  • Based on:
    Total Points ÷ (Traveler games + Tourguide games / 2). Punkt vor Strich!
🎁 Bonus Points
  • Top 10 closest guesses (year-long ranking):
    • 🥇 1st: 1000 pts
    • 🥈 2nd: 900 pts
    • 🥉 3rd: 800 pts
    • … down to 100 pts
  • Most Traveler rounds: +2500 pts
  • Most Tourguide rounds: +2500 pts
  • Most countries submitted as a Tourguide (≥3): +5000 pts
  • Each player can only win one of these three bonuses
  • If tied → everyone receives full points
🎉 Final Prize
  • The overall winner receives a surprise prize 🎁
  • Requirement:
    • At least 5 Traveler rounds
    • At least 1 Tourguide round
⚙️ Notes
  • Rules are always open to change, just let me know :). Also, soon you will be able to rate locations on beauty, uniqueness, and difficulty. Stay tuned!


Have fun! 🌍✈️
